Restaurant app


A single page web application built with Node.js/Express.js/vanilla JavaScript/sqlite3/AJAX to help restaurant owners to maintain dishes, menu categories and prices. The SQLite database was seeded with the dish names, category names, pictures and prices of dishes as well as category name. Displayed the menu categories, dishes under the categories.
